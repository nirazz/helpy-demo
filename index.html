Browse

Swipe to Discover Requests

Browse help requests from your neighbors with Tinder-style swipe cards. Each card shows the request details, distance, urgency, and photos.

  • Swipe right to offer help, left to skip
  • Distance calculated in real-time (Haversine)
  • Filter by radius: 1-25 miles or kilometers
  • 12 categories: Moving, Pets, Tech, Elderly Care, and more
  • 3 urgency levels with visual indicators
Post

Ask for Help in Seconds

Create a help request with a title, description, category, and urgency level. Add up to 3 photos to show what you need help with.

  • Quick form with category picker
  • Photo upload from camera or gallery
  • Content filter for safe community
  • Phone verification required before posting
Chat

Real-Time Messaging

Once matched, coordinate with your neighbor through instant messaging. Chat is powered by Supabase Realtime WebSocket subscriptions.

  • Instant message delivery
  • "I'm Helping" and "Helping Me" tabs
  • Complete session and rate from chat
  • Report or block users for safety
Manage

Track Your Requests

View all your active and past requests. Accept or decline offers from helpers, close completed requests, and rate your experience.

  • Active and History tabs
  • Accept or decline helper offers
  • Close requests when done
  • Rating prompt on completion
Profile

Your Community Profile

View your community impact, adjust settings, toggle availability, and switch between English and Hebrew with full RTL support.

  • Community Impact: Helped, Got Help, Rating, Posted
  • Verification badge for Google/Apple sign-in
  • Search radius and availability toggle
  • Account deletion and support contact

🇮🇱 Hebrew

Full RTL Support

Every screen, button, and label is translated. The entire layout flips to right-to-left for Hebrew speakers. Distances switch from miles to kilometers.

  • One-tap language toggle on Profile
  • All 5 tabs + headers translated
  • Navigation arrows flip for RTL
  • Hebrew uses km instead of miles
